Core Technologies

  • 🔧 Hybrid Power Unit: 1.6L V6 turbo + MGU-K/H for 1,000+ hp bursts
  • ✈️ Aerodynamics: Ground effect floors create massive downforce without dirty air
  • 🛡️ Halo & Safety: Titanium bar survives 50G impacts
  • 🛠️ Chassis and Material : Carbon fiber monocoques form lightweight, crash-resistant structures, often reinforced with Kevlar or Zylon for safety. Teams use finite element analysis (FEA) for structural integrity, balancing low weight with high strength
  • 📡 Electronics and Data : Advanced telemetry and data analysis for performance optimization
  • ⚙️ Tires : Advanced rubber compounds for optimal grip and durability
  • 🔧 Suspension : Double wishbone setups with push/pull rods, torsion bars, and active dampers manage 5+ tonnes of downforce

2026 Revolution

FIA's new rules: Smaller, lighter cars (30% less mass), 50/50 ICE-electric power, sustainable fuels, active aero for closer racing.[web:57] Expect nimble machines hitting 400 km/h straights!
